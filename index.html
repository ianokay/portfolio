BintuWronged is a retro 3D platformer game made by me and two other classmates for a capstone project.

For this project, I created all aspects of the games character models, including animations.

Additional to the characters, I created several particle systems, designed the platforming levels, and did some of the asset modeling.

We decided to each give ourselves a challenge with the project, only having 3 months to complete it.

I decided to teach myself how to use Blender, learning how to character model, rig, and animate.

Art and Technology

I was brought up in a very creative home, drawing and making crafts just about every day. My first introduction to technology was our family computer. My interest sharpened when I saw the possibilities of what I could make.

2002-2015

Video Games

I soon found interest in playing video games and the second best thing to me was making them. I would spend hours after school experimenting with websites like Scratch and I would start to create levels for flash games and post them online.

2016-2020

Combined interests
